## TrendVista — Project Overview (Public)

### One-liner
TrendVista helps GTA local businesses **spot emerging customer trends** on social media and turn them into **clear actions** (what to promote, what to add/adjust, what customers love/hate).

### The problem
Small businesses don’t have time (or a data team) to continuously monitor multiple social platforms, interpret sentiment, and identify trend shifts early enough to act.

### The solution
TrendVista automates the pipeline:
- **Collect** relevant public chatter (posts/comments/mentions) across multiple platforms
- **Process** it with NLP (sentiment + keywords + trend signals)
- **Surface** insights in a dashboard built for non-technical operators

### Target users
- Coffee shops, dessert shops, small restaurants, cafes (initial focus: GTA)

### Core capabilities
- **Trend detection**: what topics/flavors/items are rising and falling
- **Sentiment analysis**: positive/neutral/negative breakdowns with confidence scoring
- **Insights feed**: recent findings, filters by date/platform/topic
- **Dashboard visualizations**: trend lines and breakdown charts for quick decisions

### What’s private (and why)
TrendVista is intended for commercial use, so we keep these proprietary:
- production scraping/collection logic and vendor integrations
- internal ranking/aggregation details
- deployment configuration and secrets management

This public repo focuses on **architecture, decisions, and product depth**.
