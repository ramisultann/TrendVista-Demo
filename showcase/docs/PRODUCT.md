## Product walkthrough (Public)

### Who it’s for
Owners/operators of small food & beverage businesses who need **quick, reliable signals** about what customers are talking about this week—without manually monitoring multiple platforms.

### Main flows
- **Sign in / onboarding**
  - Business profile and preferences (location focus, categories, keywords)
- **Dashboard**
  - High-level KPIs (trend velocity, sentiment split, top topics)
  - Platform breakdowns and date-range filters
- **Trends**
  - “Rising / Falling” topics with evidence (representative posts)
  - Filters by platform, time window, and category
- **Insights**
  - A feed of extracted insights (topic + sentiment + confidence + timestamp)

### What makes it useful
- Designed to answer: “**What should I promote or change this week?**”
- Emphasis on **actionable** outputs (not raw analytics for analysts)
